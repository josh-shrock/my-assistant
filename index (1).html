How to export from Google Calendar
This takes about 60 seconds:
  1. Go to calendar.google.com
  2. Click the ⚙ gear icon → Settings
  3. In the left sidebar, click your calendar name under "My calendars"
  4. Scroll to Export calendar → click Export
  5. Unzip the downloaded file — you'll get a .ics file
  6. Come back here and click Import .ics file
